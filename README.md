# 🚀 Product Case Study: Amplified Yektapay Credit Funnel
*Simplified Access, Amplified Results*

Welcome! This repository contains the strategic breakdown of how we optimized the Yektapay credit application funnel, balancing user experience with core business metrics. 

---

## 🎯 The Challenge
The original credit application process was suffering from severe friction, leading to high abandonment rates. Key bottlenecks included:
* **High Cognitive Load:** A lengthy upfront identity verification process acted as the first major drop-off point.
* **Complex Funnel:** A multi-layered, model-confined application process that broke the journey for even the most determined users.
* **Critically Low Satisfaction:** A baseline Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT) sitting at just **25%**.

## 🧠 Product Strategy & Approach
To turn this around, the product strategy shifted toward removing barriers while aligning user motivations with business goals. Key initiatives included:
* **Simplifying Access:** Structuring the flow to reduce cognitive load and simplify the identity verification steps.
* **Continuous Iteration:** Relying on data-driven monitoring to quickly identify and fix broken flows.
* **Organizational Alignment:** Fostering a product culture that values empathy and measurable outcomes.

## 📈 The Impact (Key Results)
By simplifying the user journey, we achieved immediate and measurable business growth:
* 🏆 **CSAT Surge:** Increased overall user satisfaction from **25% to 78%**.
* 📉 **Funnel Optimization:** Drastic reduction in user drop-off across the entire credit application funnel.
* ⚡ **Time-to-Value:** Significant reduction in the overall time required for sign-up and request submission.
* 🚀 **Conversion Leap:** A massive increase in user entry into the main credit application process.
